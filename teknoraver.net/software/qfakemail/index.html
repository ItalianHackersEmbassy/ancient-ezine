QFakeMail

Home | Download | Comments

Send anonymous or fake emails

Have you ever wanted to send an email from a fake address, or even an anonymous one?
QFakemail allows you to do both easily,
just fill in the sender and receiver address, the message body and then send it, even with files attached

Download

QFakemail is an opensource program developed with the Qt toolkit.
